Cancellation Policy

At DigiFashionEcomm, we strive to process orders quickly. If you need to cancel an order, please contact us as soon as possible.

Timeframe for Cancellation

Before Shipping: You can cancel your order for a full refund at any time before it has been shipped. Please check your order status on your account page or contact our support team at Digifashionecommerce@gmail.com.

How to Cancel

To cancel an order, please go to your 'My Orders' page (once available) and select the 'Cancel Order' option. If the option is not available, it means your order is already being processed for shipping. In this case, please contact us immediately.

After Shipping

If your order has already been shipped, it cannot be canceled. However, you can return the order once it is delivered. Please refer to our Return Policy (once available) for details on how to return an item.

Refunds

All refunds for canceled orders will be processed to the original form of payment within 5-7 business days. If you paid via QR code/UPI, the refund will be credited back to the source account.
